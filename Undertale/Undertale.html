    What is Undertale?

    Undertale is an RPG video game in which the choices you make as the player greatly impacts your gaming experience.

    This game can be played on a PC, Mac, Linux, Vita, and PS4.

    Your

    Character

    Who do You Play As?

    You play a character who has fallen down Mt. Ebott, and is trapped down in the realm of monsters.

    In order to be able to escape, you would encounter fights with monsters, and various situations that would challenge you.

    Different

    Ways to

    Play

    What Choices can You Make?

    "In this world, it's kill, or BE killed."

    Or is it?

    There are three ways to play:
    - The Pacifist Route - The Genocide Route - The Neutral Route

    Each route presents a unique way of gameplay and challenges particular to the route you choose.

    The

    Outcome

    What is the Outcome of Your Actions?

    The Pacifist Route involves taking action in a way that doesn't harm any character you encounter. This means that there is no killing involved in the gameplay.
    The Genocide Route involves killing every character you encounter in battle.
    The Neutral Route involves a mix of both. You can essentially play both routes in one, by sparing some monsters,
    and killing others.

    If you start your gameplay with genocide, your actions will be remembered throughout, as well as into the next time you start the game. Characters will remember what actions you had taken during previous playthroughs.
    The same can be said for the other routes you choose.

    Be aware of the consequences your actions have...

    Fight

    Mechanics

    What Are the Fighting Mechanics When You Encounter an Attack?

    When you encounter a monster, an exclamation mark will pop up, and you would go into battle with them. There are many options to choose from. You can FIGHT, which means you'll be able to attack the monster, and maybe even kill them. You can ACT. There are actions you can take that are particular to a specific monster. Or you can SPARE, which is when a monster doesn't want to fight anymore. You can show them MERCY, and leave them be.
